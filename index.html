Our Story

Born on the Mountains

The Rising Stars was founded by a group of passionate trekkers from Nagpur who believed that every person deserves to experience the magic of the mountains — safely and affordably.

What started as weekend treks with friends has grown into Maharashtra's most trusted adventure company, with 250+ successful expeditions and 1000+ lives transformed on the trail.
